**NovaTrade Terminal** is not just a currency converter; it's a professional-grade financial workspace that fits in your pocket. Inspired by the Bloomberg Terminal aesthetics, this app combines high-performance rendering with the intelligence of Google's Gemini AI.

Whether you are tracking market volatility, asking "Robovan" for financial advice, or using voice commands to convert currencies hands-free, NovaTrade delivers a premium experience.

* **Framework:** Flutter (Dart)
